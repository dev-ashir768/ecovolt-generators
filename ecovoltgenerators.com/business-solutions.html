Custom Solutions for Business Savings

Paralleling

Paralleling up to 32 generator systems, diesel or gaseous, produces enhanced redundancy,
lower capital costs and long term savings to the customer.

Paralleling equates to:

  • Less maintenance costs
  • No downtime
  • Fuel Savings

Peak Shaving

Businesses can be charged penalties for high electrical use during periods of high demand on the energy grid.
Utilizing generator power can replace or supplement grid power during peak production periods to avoid costly penalties.
At Sommers, we will assess your needs to provide recommendations and support you through
installation, maintenance and emergency support.

Developing your "Power Profile" with Eyedro

  • Consult utility bills for actual consumption patterns
  • Measure peak loads throughout the day
  • Choose the standby power YOU need

Global Adjustment Abatement

Global Adjustment (GA) costs charged by utilities are assessed according to energy consumption during specific sampling periods. GA Abatement strategies use off-grid power to reduce demand during the assessment dates.
Sommers can implement GA Abatement strategies tailored to any facility:

  • Supply and maintain rental systems during target periods
  • Recommend and train facility staff to operate purchased or leased equipment
  • “Power by the hour” turnkey solutions in collaboration with the local utility
