Off-Grid Hybrid Systems

"Our customer was able to disconnect permanently from the grid and now operates with a fully integrated hybrid solar power solution."

Earlier this summer, a wind-storm brought down the poles & power lines that fed this customer's property. Our engineers worked closely with the client to build a custom hybrid system, specific to their needs. The customer was able to disconnect permanently from the grid and now operates their facility with a fully integrated hybrid solar power solution. This system utilizes a Sommers 10kW diesel generator combined with solar panels and a SimpliPhi power energy storage and power management system. Distributed in Canada by Sommers Generator Systems, SimpliPhi Power, Inc. products are an economical off-grid and emergency backup solution, easy to install and expandable as your demands grow.

Applicable For Any Situation

All over the world, generators are used as back up power for homes.

Combining PHI batteries with your generator can create fuel savings and extend your available reserves, as well as cut down on environmental and noise pollution.

SimpliPhi's turn-key residential solutions such as the AccESS and PowerBank have a built-in automatic generator start (AGS) to optimize your generator's performance with grid and/or PHI battery power.
‍
Time of Use Electrical Usage:
Homeowners who are on a time-of use-rate or an electric vehicle rate, can charge PHI batteries when energy is cheaper (for example at night) and discharge energy when energy is more expensive. By enabling homeowners to shift their use of grid electricity to less expensive times, SimpliPhi storage can significantly reduce utility bills.

‍Using Solar?
Adding SimpliPhi storage to your solar array stores excess power for you to access during an emergency. Storing excess solar power can also provide a greater return on your renewable energy investment, particularly in regions where utilities have unfavorable rate structures, such as demand and time of use charges. PHI batteries also allow you to draw from your storage for power at night and during times of inclement weather. Combining solar plus storage allows you to generate and consume your own power on demand, rather than relying solely on the utility.
