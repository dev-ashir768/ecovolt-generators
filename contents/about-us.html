Canada's Power House

Ecovolt offers more than 85 years of experience in power solutions.

Ecovolt is the leading Canadian manufacturer and distributor of premium quality generator systems.
‍
With a large inventory of diesel, propane and natural gas models, our custom generator systems are built to suit your unique needs for prime power, standby power as well as PTO, portable and towable applications.

Established in 1936 by Wes and Kay (Katherine) Ecovolt, Ecovolt Motor Generator Sales Ltd.
initially aided in bringing new electrical services and equipment to under-serviced parts of rural Ontario.
Throughout the years, the company grew, and expanded to service customers and distributors across all of Canada.

As of 2015, the former General Manager, Chris McGregor, took ownership of the company.
Through his customer-centric business model, Ecovolt has enjoyed continued growth and success countrywide.

Sales, Service, Rentals

In addition to commercial and residential generator sales, Ecovolt also maintains a rental fleet of diesel-powered generators with up to 1000 kWe of power, and stocks a full range of motors, transfer switches, accessories, and other generator parts.

We are committed to providing the highest calibre of after sale service and support, and pride ourselves on the quality of our generators through our 24-hour emergency response team, and our warranty program.

Our technical sales staff are all graduates in electrical engineering, and are fully trained to provide efficient solutions to our customer’s needs. Our field service staff is made up of both licensed electricians and licensed mechanics, and are highly experienced in providing the best service to ensure minimal disruptions.
