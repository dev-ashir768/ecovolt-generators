Canada’s Power House

Ecovolt brings decades of expertise in delivering reliable power solutions across Canada. As a leading Canadian manufacturer and distributor of premium-quality generator systems, we provide tailored solutions designed to meet diverse needs.
With a wide range of diesel, propane, and natural gas models, our custom-built systems are engineered for prime power, standby power, as well as PTO, portable, and towable applications.
From supporting communities to powering industries, Ecovolt has grown into a trusted partner nationwide. Guided by a customer-first approach, we continue to expand, delivering dependable energy solutions that keep Canada moving.

Sales, Service, Rentals

Along with providing commercial and residential generator solutions, Ecovolt offers a reliable rental fleet of diesel-powered generators delivering up to 1000 kWe of power. We also stock a complete range of motors, transfer switches, accessories, and generator parts to meet every requirement.
Our commitment goes beyond sales — we deliver exceptional after-sale support backed by a 24/7 emergency response team and an industry-leading warranty program.
Ecovolt’s technical sales team consists of highly trained electrical engineering graduates who ensure efficient, tailored solutions for every client. Our field service experts include licensed electricians and mechanics with extensive hands-on experience, ensuring smooth operation and minimal downtime for your power systems.
