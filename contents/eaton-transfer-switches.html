Eaton Transfer Switches

Ecovolt Generators offers Eaton automatic transfer switches (ATS) that are designed to quickly and reliably transition critical loads between preferred and alternate/generator power sources.

Eaton’s Bypass Isolation Transfer Switches include dual automatic switching mechanisms that provide redundancy for critical applications. The ATS distributes electrical power to the load, while the automatic bypass serves as a backup.
